Uxcell 3Pcs Durable Zinc Alloy Door Stoppers

Keep your doors safely open with our reliable and robust door stoppers designed for everyday use. Perfect for homes, offices, and commercial spaces, these stoppers combine strength with silent functionality to protect your property and loved ones.

Durable and Strong

Constructed from high-quality zinc alloy, these door stoppers are built to last. Their sturdy build helps prevent doors from slamming shut or closing unexpectedly, offering peace of mind and added safety for both people and possessions.

Versatile and Easy to Install

With an adjustable K-type design, these stoppers fit most door sizes and are easy to install. The hinge pin system ensures compatibility with standard door hardware, while the compact size (55 x 45mm) keeps them unobtrusive yet effective in your space.

Silent and Safe Operation

The rubber bumper tips on each stopper provide a quiet and gentle stop, preventing loud noises and potential damage to door edges. They also help protect fingers from pinching and reduce the risk of accidents in busy areas.

Built for Longevity

These stoppers are specially designed to resist corrosion and maintain structural integrity even with frequent use. Their reliable performance ensures they are a long-term solution for keeping doors open and safe in high-traffic environments.

Perfect for Every Space

Whether you're looking for an adjustable door stop, a silent door stop, or a durable zinc alloy door stop, these stoppers are the ideal choice. They are also great for hinge pin door stop installations and offer a versatile, safe, and stylish solution for all door types.
